How to Make the Perfect Iced Latte




Step 1

Check Water Tank

Water tank should be at least half full. If it's not, add more

Step 2

Prepare Glass

Put three to four ice cubes in the bottom of glass and place glass in place under the Nespresso machine.

Step 3

Put Pod in Machine

Lift the lid, place the pod, and close the lid

Step 4

Press Button

Repeat steps 3 and 4 for how many shots of espresso you want

Step 5

Flavor (optional)

Add flavoring syrup of your choice to your glass. I like to do two or three pumps.

Step 6

Milk

Fill rest of glass with milk or milk substitute of your choice.

Step 7

Stir

Mix everything together and enjoy!
